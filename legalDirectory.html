Family & Domestic Violence

Are you, or someone you know, in danger?

If the danger is immediate, please call 000 now.

For non-urgent help, you can contact the Queensland police at 131 444.

Show Additional Helpful Services
  • Victim Assist Queensland, a support service provided by the Queensland Government, available during business hours: 1300 546 587
  • DV Connect Womensline, a 24/7 Domestic Violence Helpline for women: 1800 811 811
  • DV Connect Mensline, a Domestic Violence Helpline for men available from 09:00-Midnight 7 days a week: 1800 600 636
  • DV Connect Sexual Assault Helpline, a Sexual Assault Helpline for victims or persons concerned about someone they know, available from 07:30-23:30 7 days a week: 1800 010 120
